Devices

Devices are custom blocks that our Machines & Tech plugin, Minetorio, adds in. Devices all of the following traits in common.

  • Input Slots - Inventory Slots that devices will look at to use as ingredients
  • Output Slots - Inventory Slots that devices use to place their output item in
  • Device Information - A Item that shows useful information about the device
  • Produce / Action Time - The time it tacks to do one device action.
